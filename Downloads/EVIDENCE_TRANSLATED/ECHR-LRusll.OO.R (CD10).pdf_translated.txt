Dear Madam,
I inform you that your complaint filed on June 27, 2011 and
Registered under the above number, was considered.

participation of the speaker, in accordance with Article 24 § 2 of the Convention) in the period from November 14, 2013

set forth in Articles 34 and 35 of the Convention.

Ward), in no other organ. I also inform that the court secretariat cannot provide
