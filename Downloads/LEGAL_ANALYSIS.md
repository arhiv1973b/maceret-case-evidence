# LEGAL ANALYSIS - MACERET V. MOLDOVA

## Art. 3 ECHR Violation - Torture

### Substantive Prohibition

> "No one shall be subjected to torture or to inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment."

### Application to Maceret Case

1. **Torture (17-18.07.1997)**
   - Victim tied up and beaten with rubber baton
   - Police station Riscany, Moldova
   - Perpetrator: Dulche V.G.

2. **Inhuman Treatment**
   - False imprisonment
   - Framed criminal charges

3. **Degrading Treatment**
   - Forced to sign false statements
   - Continuous harassment

### Victim Vulnerability (Art. 3)

- **Group I Disability** (established)
- Previous torture victim
- No legal protection

---

## Art. 6 ECHR Violation - Fair Trial

### Fair Hearing

- False evidence used
- No independent investigation
- Judge conflict of interest

### Defense Rights Violated

- No access to evidence
- No witness examination
- No proper appeal
